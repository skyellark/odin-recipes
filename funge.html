Description

Funge is a common dish in Angola, usually accompanied by other local delicacies, you could say that funge is the Angolan equivalent of rice in Japan

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cassava Flour

Equipment

  • Pot
  • Muxarico
  1. Fist, get yourself a pot of any kind or size depenging on how much funge you want.
  2. Then, you put the water inside but don't let it boil as of yet.
  3. Add small amounts of the cassava flour to the pot while you use your muxarico to "beat" the funge
